Rakuten
Cashback
Every time you shop online — Amazon, Target, Nike, Walmart, anywhere — Rakuten pays you a percentage back automatically. No coupons. No codes. You just shop the way you normally would and money shows up in your account every quarter. Members average $150+ back per year just from purchases they were already making.
Free • Browser extension + app • rakuten.com
Ibotta
Grocery Cash
Ibotta gives you cashback on groceries at virtually any store — Walmart, Kroger, Aldi, Costco, you name it. Scan your receipt after shopping and cash out via PayPal or Venmo. No brand switching required. People who use it consistently pull $20–$50 back per month just on their normal grocery run.
Free • iOS & Android • ibotta.com
Acorns
Micro-Invest
Acorns rounds up every purchase you make to the nearest dollar and invests the difference automatically. Buy a coffee for $4.60, Acorns invests $0.40. It sounds small but it adds up — the average user invests $30–$50 per month without thinking about it. Your spare change gets put into a diversified portfolio and grows over time.
Free to start • iOS & Android • acorns.com
Rocket Money
Kill Subscriptions
The average American wastes $219 per month on subscriptions they forgot they signed up for. Rocket Money scans your bank account, finds every recurring charge, and cancels the ones you don't want — with one tap. It also negotiates your bills (cable, internet, phone) on your behalf and keeps a cut of the savings only if it works.
Free tier available • iOS & Android • rocketmoney.com
